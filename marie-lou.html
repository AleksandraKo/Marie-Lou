Marie-Lou Sohnius

Bachelor Student

University of Mannheim

I am a Bachelor student and student research assistant at the University of Mannheim. My research interests include voting behaviour, public opinion, political campaigns and Social Media effects. I am also interested in Global Political Economy studies where I focus on the study of neoliberalism and racial inequality. My work on public opinion and voting behaviour has been published in the Tagesspiegel and the DVPW.
Furthermore I am active at Enactus Mannheim e.V., a student organisation tackling global problems through social entrepreneurship. In 2018/19 I was member of the board and leader of the public relations department. Since July 2019, I support the organisation in an advisory role.
